## Frequently Asked Questions

**How long does it take to build a website?**
Most projects go from kickoff to launch in about 14 days. Premium/complex projects may take 3-4 weeks.

**Do I own my website?**
Yes, 100%. Your code, your design, your content, your domain. No lock-in, no hostage situations.

**What if I just want a website and nothing else?**
Absolutely. Every service can be purchased individually. Packages are just there to save you money if you want multiple services.

**How does payment work?**
50% upfront to start, 50% on launch. Monthly services are billed monthly with a 3-month minimum commitment.

**Do I get a professional email with my website?**
We set up email redirects so you can receive mail at a professional address like info@yourbusiness.com — it forwards straight to your existing email. This is included with hosting.

**Can I cancel monthly services?**
After the initial 3-month commitment, you can cancel any monthly service with 30 days notice.

**Do you work with businesses outside Tampa Bay?**
We specialize in Pinellas and Pasco County, but we're happy to work with businesses anywhere in Florida.
